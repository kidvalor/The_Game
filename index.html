Why Did The Chicken Cross The Road?

To Start Game press Start Button and press Up Arrow Key.

Make it across to the black square without being hit by a car and without touching the water.

There is a total of 3 rounds to win, each round the cars and logs move faster. You only have 2 lives before Game Over. Can you make it over?


Controls:

  • Up = Up Arrow
  • Down = Down Arrow
  • Left = Left Arrow
  • Right = Right Arrow




Seconds left: 20


Lives left: 2

Round: 1
